The Heinicke Dive

In a sensational turn of events, Taylor Heinicke, the emergency COVID backup was brought in as the starter in a playoff scenario when QB's Alex Smith and Dwayne Haskins went down with injury. The former ODU student threw scored three touchdowns and this was the pick of the bunch! With the pocket crumbling around him he managed to Houdini out popping free, beating the DB's to the pylon with a superhuman stretch to the goal.
